    FAQ

    Can I order food out of town?

    Yes, our service offers delivery at any place all over the country! One the biggest plus of using Tessa is that we do not fear the distance. You just need to know what would you like to eat and our curriers will deliver it at any place! Also, we work 24 hours 7 days a week!

    How fast does the delivery service work?

    Delivery time varies from 10-50mins, depending on your location and how far you are from your favorite restaurant! We are trying our best to deliver your dish as soon as possible. Delivering food while it's still hot is our main priority. Therefore, rapid service is our signature!
